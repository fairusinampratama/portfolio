# Content Requirements

This document defines what must be prepared. It intentionally does not contain final case-study copy.

## Voice and editorial rules

- Calm, direct, specific, first-person where ownership is confirmed.
- Explain business context before technology.
- Prefer verbs such as mapped, designed, implemented, integrated, reconciled, tested, deployed, and troubleshot.
- Avoid “passionate,” “innovative,” “cutting-edge,” “robust,” “seamless,” “high-performance,” and “production-ready” unless a concrete explanation follows.
- Use short paragraphs, descriptive headings, and evidence captions.
- Every status/scale statement must have a source and approved wording.
